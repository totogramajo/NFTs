Satoshi Nakamoto says it was a hot and sunny morning when Bob spotted the first HUKKA settlement in the middle of an Oasis lost in the vast Qatari desert. His reaction was consistent with the magnitude of the discovery: the McDonald brothers, his loyal traveling companions, affirm that they had never seen, nor did they see again, Bob amazed like that. Years traveling around more than 150 countries, thousands of cities visited and countless cultures absorbed, were not enough to prepare Bob for what was in front of his eyes.

From the first moment, he knew that he was approaching a civilization totally unknown to the rest of humanity. The McDonald brothers were quick to compare him to Christopher Columbus as they became aware of the majestic event they were witnessing.

A young ghost named La'eeb' escorted them to the island while Bob wrote everything down in his legendary travel log. A nomadic community made up of a conspicuous fusion of foreigners who smiled everywhere and seemed extremely wealthy at first glance: those were the HUKKAS. There were citizens from all over the world, who although they all came from completely different cultures, they all shared an obsessive passion for sports. A multicultural culture based on the love of sport. "Yes, that's what HUKKAS are '' said Bob as he lit his cigarette imported precisely from V-City, the secret capital of the HUKKA Empire.

About halfway around V-City, the travelers found themselves before an imposing temple called MIZUKI. As ancient as it is majestic, Bob couldn't help but remember his visits to the mythical Taj Mahal when he was just a young novice. A few moments later, Laebe instructed his guests to pay attention to the HUKKAS coming in and out of MIZUKI. What they beheld was overwhelming: every HUKKA that entered in MIZUKI, left with bags of money and unmistakably new clothing. At that precise moment, Bob understood that the HUKKA economy was founded on a fun P2E, where the HUKKAS capitalized on their passion for gaming. MIZUKI is to the HUKKAS what Wall Street is to the Stock Exchange, everything happens in MIZUKI. At MIZUKI, the HUKKAS not only earn money but also participate in the huge marketplace that is available for them. When we talk about MIZUKI, we cannot forget about the great Jackpot that offers rewards for all the HUKKAS that manage to accomplish the weekly goals. In this way, the highest HUKKA ideal based on granting opportunities for all members of the community materializes.

Speaking with a HUKKA of Ghanaian origin, Bob learned that a very common practice among HUKKAS is to play P2P modes where the winner of each game wins magical chests offered on the market. In turn, these magical chests contain luxurious clothing, which not only provide a better appearance but also mean a notable prestige within the community. This is how the influence in the HUKKA community changes of hands constantly, without any of them being able to impose themselves as a permanent leader. Play to win - Win to grow - Grow to be. This is how the Hukkas lived for several years, going unnoticed by the rest of the world.

But what was it that triggered the appearance of this eccentric community with people from all over the world? Asked Bob who still couldn't take his eyes off the great MIZUKI. The World Cup, Laebe replied, we all look forward to the Qatar 2022 World Cup. Upon hearing this and without taking his eyes off him, Bob entered MIZUKI hypnotized, understanding that this was the beginning of a path of prosperity and wealth that exceeds even his own limits of imagination.

Bob is a HUKKA. The McDonald brothers are HUKKAS. We are all HUKKAS, only some take longer to discover it. And you? Are you a HUKKA?
