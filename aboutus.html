What we do?

We started out as internet kids armed with a diehard love for video games. We've turned our passions and struggles into a global creative engine that has been a part of defining the gaming culture.

V-SPORTS is at the center of gaming, sports, and culture, challenging how next generations will consume, play and shop.

We created our own ecosystem through the blockchain mechanics, where users can earn money while having fun playing.

V-SPORTS offers a unique NFTS play to earn game experience.

We are a company dedicated to NFTS Play-And-Earn games, focusing on sports. We are going to take out several collections, the first one is based on the Soccer World Cup. It has a number of "4,320" NFTS players.

We seek to bring back the traditional fashions of global gaming. First stage "SOCCER", game simple, entertaining and easy to play.

NFTs in letter format, which can be used in our Play-And-Earn, can only be entered by users who verify that they are HOLDERS of our collection.

Rewards and benefits vary depending on the "RARITY" of the NFT (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Legendary, and Mythic).
